Miso Eggplant

Description

Estimated time: 30 minutes

Servings: 2-3

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 onion sliced into strips
  • 4 Chinese or Japanese eggplants sliced
  • 2 tbsps of miso paste
  • 1 tbsp of brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp of mirin
  • 2 tbsps of canola oil
  • water

Steps

  1. Mix the miso, brown sugar, and mirin into a bowl
  2. Pour a little bit of water, enough so that the mixture becomes a soft paste
  3. Heat the canola oil on a pan at low heat
  4. At medium high heat, saut√©e the garlic and onion until they start to become brown
  5. Add the eggplant until it starts to become soft
  6. Add the miso mixture onto the eggplant and mix them well
  7. Simmer for 6 minutes
