Honey Miso Chicken

Description

Estimated time: 1 day (marinate) / 16 minutes (cook)

Servings: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 3 thinly sliced boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsps of miso paste
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 2 tbsps of canola oil

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts inside a ziploc bag
  2. Add the miso paste and honey into the ziploc bag. Mix well with the chicken
  3. Place the ziploc bag in the fridge until the following day to marinate
  4. Heat up the canola oil on a frying pan at low heat
  5. At medium high heat, place the chicken breasts on the pan and leave it cooking for 8 minutes
  6. Flip the chicken and cook the other side for 8 minutes or until its internal temperature reaches 165 Â°F
